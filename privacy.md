# Privacy Policy — Khata Manager Pro

**Last updated:** June 7, 2026

This privacy policy explains how Khata Manager Pro ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information when you use our desktop and mobile applications.

## 1. Data Storage — All Local

Khata Manager Pro stores all your data **locally on your device**. We do not operate any servers, databases, or cloud storage. Your business data — including customer records, transactions, inventory, and bills — never leaves your device unless you explicitly export or back it up.

| Data type | Where it's stored |
|-----------|-------------------|
| Customer ledgers, transactions, products | Local SQLite database (`khata_manager_pro.db`) |
| App settings & theme preferences | Local Hive storage |
| Backups (encrypted) | Your chosen export location (local file system) |

## 2. No Account, No Registration

The app does **not** require an account, email, phone number, or any personal information to function. There is no sign-up, login, or cloud sync.

## 3. Advertisements

The free version displays ads via **Google AdSense** served inside a WebView component. Google may collect anonymized usage data for ad personalization as described in [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). You can opt out of personalized ads via your [Google Ad Settings](https://adssettings.google.com/).

## 4. Permissions Used

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| **Camera** (mobile only) | Scanning product barcodes. Only used when you actively tap the scan button. No images are stored or transmitted. |
| **Internet** | Loading advertisements (AdSense via WebView). Core features work fully offline. |
| **File access** | Exporting/importing Excel files, creating PDF bills, and saving encrypted backups — only when you explicitly initiate these actions. |

## 5. Third-Party Services

- **Google AdSense** — Serves ads through a WebView. See Google's privacy policy for their data practices.
- **Microsoft Store** (Windows version) — Standard Store telemetry as described in Microsoft's privacy statement.

## 6. Data Backup & Export

When you create a backup or export data:
- The file is saved to a location **you choose** on your device
- Backups are **encrypted** (XOR + SHA-256 checksum)
- You are responsible for any copies you share or store externally

## 7. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for business users and small business owners. It does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
